ESG Impact: Camp Responsibly

Camp Responsibly: Repair, Return & Reuse

SolarLink turns the booth into a responsible camping initiative that reduces waste and builds community trust.

  1. Visitors bring old camping gear or damaged tents to the booth.
  2. SolarLink collects usable materials for repair, reuse, or recycling.
  3. Participants receive a reward or product discount.
  4. The booth uses reusable decorations and solar-powered lighting.
  5. Accessible seating and charging are available for all visitors.
